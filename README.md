**MastUi** is your personal AI design architect. It solves the "ugly UI" problem by taking any raw screenshot and completely reimagining it with world-class aesthetics. 

## ✨ What MastUi Does

1. **AI Vision Analysis:** It "sees" your interface, identifying all buttons, inputs, and layouts while maintaining their functional integrity.
2. **Style Transformation:** It applies advanced design systems—from sleek **Glassmorphism** to futuristic **Cyberpunk**—in seconds.
3. **Professional Redesign:** It automatically injects fluid gradients, soft shadows, and high-end typography to elevate your design to an artist-level masterpiece.
4. **Instant Export:** Once your design is ready, you can download it as a high-resolution blueprint for your next big project.

## 🎨 Available Design Philosophies

- **Mast:** Buttery smooth glassmorphism and minimalist trends.
- **Neumorphism:** Tactile, soft UI with organic depth and shadows.
- **Brutalism:** Bold, raw, and high-contrast editorial aesthetics.
- **Cyberpunk:** Neon-infused digital HUDs and high-tech vibes.
- **Story:** Cinematic, narrative-driven layouts for editorial elegance.
- **Artist:** Abstract, unconventional, and expressive digital art.
